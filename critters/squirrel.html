Squirrel

Info

The squirrel companions can be found in the Plaza and have five color variations. Their favorite food is peanuts. Other foods they like are apples, bananas, blueberries, cherries, cocoa beans, coconut, coffee beans, Dreamlight fruit, egg-cellent fruit, gooseberries, lemon, and raspberries.

How to Feed Them

To feed them the Player just has to walk up to them and interact with them. The squirrels won't run from the Player or play any games.
