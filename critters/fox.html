Fox

Info

The fox companions can be found in the Frosted Heights and have five color variations. Their favorite food is white sturgeon and other foods they like are bass, crab, Here and There fish, salmon, and tilapia.

How to Feed Them

To feed them the Player must play a game of 'tag'! Run towards them once they are looking at you playfully and chase after them until they stop running away. Once the fox is sitting down the chase is over.
