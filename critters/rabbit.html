Disney Dreamlight Valley Companion

Rabbit

Info

The rabbit companions can be found in the Peaceful Meadow and have five color variations. Their favorite food is carrots, which can sometimes be found in Goofy's stall in the Peaceful Meadow or the Player can buy carrot seeds from him at the same stall. Other foods they like are asparagus, basil, bell pepper, canola, chili pepper, corn, cucumber, eggplant, garlic, ginger, leek, lettuce, mushroom, okra, onion, oregano, potato, pumpkin, rice, spinach, spring V-EGG-etable, soya, sugarcane, tomato, vanilla, wheat, and zucchini.

How to Feed Them

To feed them the Player must play a game of 'Tag'! Run towards them once they are looking at you and jumping up and down, and chase after them until they stop running away. Once the rabbit is sitting down the chase is over.
