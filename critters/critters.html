Critters

The Critters in Disney Dreamlight Valley are small companions that the Player can equip in their wardrobe after they have been befriended.

To befriend a critter, simply feed it a food it loves or likes on a day and time it's available. The ones that are currently available can be seen on the daily schedule below. For information regarding what food the critters like, please see the individual critter type's guide.

Critters can drop Dream Shards, Memories, Motif Bags, and other things such as seeds and Iron Ingots. Only the first feed of a day for the specific critter will drop a reward. Once befriended, the Player can also take pictures with the critter companion, as well as place them in their valley by crafting a Cozy Companion Home.
