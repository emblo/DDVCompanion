Raven

Info

The raven companions can be found in the Forgotten Lands and have five color variations. Their favorite food is 5-star meals. Other foods they like are 3-star and 4-star meals.

How to Feed Them

To feed them the Player must walk towards them until they start circling above the Player's head. The raven will circle for some time then fly down to the Player, where they can then be interacted with.
