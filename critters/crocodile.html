Disney Dreamlight Valley Companion

Crocodile

Info

The crocodile companions can be found in the Glade of Trust and have six color variations. Their favorite food is lobster, which can be fished from gold pools in the Glade of Trust. Other foods they like are cod, Here and There fish, herring, squid, and tuna.

How to Feed Them

To feed them the Player must play a game of 'Red light, green light'. Run towards the crocodile when their head is down and immediately stop as it's up. The crocodile will pull it's head up and look around before dropping it's head again, move closer when it's down. Once the crocodile is flat on it's belly it's safe to move freely around them.
