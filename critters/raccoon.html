Raccoon

Info

The raccoon companions can be found in the Forest of Valor and have five color variations. Their favorite food is blueberries. Other foods they like are gooseberries and raspberries.

How to Feed Them

To feed them the Player must play a game of 'red light, green light'. Run towards the raccoon when they are sitting down and immediately stop as they stand up on their hind legs. The raccoon will stand up and look around before dropping down again, move closer when it's down. Once the raccoon sits down flat on its butt it's safe to move freely around them.
