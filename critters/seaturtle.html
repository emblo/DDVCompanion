Disney Dreamlight Valley Companion

Sea Turtle

Info

The sea turtle companions can be found on Dazzle Beach and have five color variations. Their favorite food is seaweed. Other foods they like are clams, crab, lobster, oysters, scallops, squid and shrimp.

How to Feed Them

To feed them the Player must walk towards them until they start hiding in their shell. The sea turtle will hide for some time then pop out of its shell, and then they can be interacted with.
