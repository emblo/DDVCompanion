The Wonder of Pixar: Event

The Wonder of Pixar Star Path began June 7th, 2023 and will end August 6th, 2023.

In this event the Player can earn Event Tokens by completing Event Duties, and the tokens can then be spent on clothing, furniture, motifs, Dream Styles, hairstyles and companions.

To purchase every event reward the Player must unlock the Premium Star Path track for 2500 Moonstones, or 3300 Moonstones where the Player also will receive 100 event tokens straight away.

In order to fully complete the event the Player must obtain all event rewards from 6 pages worth of rewards. If the Player has 10 or more event tokens left after this, there is a 7th page where the Player can trade in these tokens in return for Moonstones.
