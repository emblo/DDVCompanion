✨Welcome to Disney Dreamlight Valley Companion!✨

This is a complete companion application to the game Disney Dreamlight Valley. Here you can find information regarding the current event in the game and its rewards, the game's critters and characters and how to befriend them, as well as the crafting and cooking recipes that can be found in game.

News

The DreamSnaps update was released on July 19th and brings a whole new event to the valley! DreamSnaps allows the player to unleash their creativity in weekly outfit or decoration challenges pertaining to specific themes. The Player gets to submit their own entry for the weekly competition, as well as vote for other players submissions!

With update 1.6.0 Vanellope also arrives to the valley, bringing new friendship quests and rewards with her. Along with Vanellope and her quests and items, this update brings with it bug fixes and game improvements in order to help improve the experience in the valley.
